Goals

The European Union strives to support the physical health and well-being of its citizens. The aim of the Treasure Hunt for Health programme is to encourage an active lifestyle through participation in a transnational treasure hunt.

The Treasure Hunt for Health also provides a means for local businesses to expand their reach through partnership in the programme.

How it works

Each EU country partners with several local businesses who supply "treasure" -- this could be store merchandise, a voucher, or any other product provided by partner companies. The treasure is hidden in safe, public locations around the country.

Participation is free and open to everyone! Find clues on our website to help you track down the treasure in your area. Get walking and keep an eye out!

Once you've located the treasure, take a photo and submit it to our website. With your consent, we will share photos on our social media platforms.
